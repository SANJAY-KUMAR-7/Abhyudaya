"We cannot all succeed
when half of us are held back."

- Malala Yousafzai

Suhasini-saving smile of a girl child was founded in 2015 to create awareness towards the education of girls children in society. We believe that a girl should be prepared perfectly to face every situation of life, so we always try that despite getting proper education she should also know the rights she has since birth. So that she won't be fooled in the future anymore. We provide such knowledge by the campaigns or by having sessions in their school.

Through Suhasini, we all are trying to promote awareness for the girl child in society. We are trying to provide education to the girls and trying to create a better future for them. Moving forward we all are monitoring about 12 government schools to provide appropriate knowledge to the girls going to school. So far we have helped more than 220 girls to go to school.

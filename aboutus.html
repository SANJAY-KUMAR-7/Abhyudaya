ABHYUDAYA-The Social Arm of JECRC

WHO WE ARE

Today the world is moving ahead day by day in the field of technology, education, sports etc ,so everyone is moving along with them. But the question arises are we all moving together? The answer is a BIG NO. Many parts and sections of our society are still sufferingl, they are not aware of many facilities, the importance of education! Quite a few girls even today are burning their hands to fulfil their family wishes.

Abhyudaya - The social arm of JECRC is a collective word for the social verticals of JECRC FOUNDATION. It imbibes four Social Verticals: Aashayein, Zarurat, Suhasini and Soch under its wings. It was started with an initial strength of (number of members) volunteers having zeal and zest to augment a body, which can provide a window to the students of the institute who wish to work for the betterment of mankind. It Started with a clear cut vision to channelize the fledgling energy of the youth towards a virtuous cause.

Mission

To focus on the spirit of innovation that can become a driving force in helping and serving our society. To develop human potential to its fullest extent so that intellectually capable and imaginatively gifted individuals can become an aid in generating a ray of hope.

VISION

To create a centre full of compassion and empathy which develops an optimistic approach so that communities instil their faith in goodness.

WAY FORWARD

To approach a larger section of society and understand their needs so that strategies can be prepared accordingly to take them out from adverse situations. To ensure that our work process motivates everyone so that we all can contribute towards sustainable development.

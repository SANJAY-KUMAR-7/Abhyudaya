Online Classes and Activites

At the time of this unprecedented Covid pandemic it was an opportunity to spend time with your loved once. Zarurat- one of the social arm of Abhudaya didn't gave up its mission to educate unprivileged children. The Social Club organized online classes for the students. It was a fruitful decision and the team was able to achieve its desired targets.

Abhyudaya-The social arm of JECRC in collaboration with SAMBAL FOUNDATION organised an online virtual keynote session, intending to preach Echo Emtathy Webinar on 24th May,2021. The theme of the session revolved around empathy being the necessity in a human being's life!

Advisory Board Meeting

Online advisory board meeting is one of the most important event of Abhudaya.It is the result of the board member's invaluable guidance and motivation that we are able to achieve this credibility and we look forward to seeking the assistance in the future as well.

आत्मनिर्भर - एक समाज, एक साथ

ABHYUDAYA feels blessed and motivated by you Sir. You are an inspiration in yourself. Your words moved us and we feel immensely blessed to have you in talk series आत्मनिर्भर - एक समाज एक साथ inspiring the youth on August 19, 2020.

Alumni Meet

Team Abhyudaya organized much awaited Alumni Meet of all the four Social Initiatives ~ Aashayein, Soch, Suhasini and Zarurat on 20th of June'21. The meeting incorporated founders and alumni members of different Social Verticals, student volunteers of the groups and shared their remembrances and discussed some important points affiliated to the initiatives. It was a remarkable experience of laughter, learnings and success stories among the members ofJECRC Family.

Webinar on Women Health Amid Covid-19

An exclusive webinar session for the women was organized by team "Suhasini - An initiative to save the smile of the girl child" on the topic "Women Health amid Covid-19" scheduled on 22nd May 2021 in endeavors to provide health and nutrition tips to the women in these tough times of Covid.This important session was attended by almost all the female teachers of JECRC Foundation and JECRC University with up to 150 and above female students.

Online session for Peace and Positivity

An Interactive session held on 22nd April, 2021 on the topic- Positive Thinking & Thoughts where Rajyogini BK Poonam Didi was invited as the chief guest to motivate students in this corona pandemic.

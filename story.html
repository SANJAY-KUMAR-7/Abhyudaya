DO GOOD, GOOD WILL AUTOMATICALLY COME TO YOU

Kshma Sharma, one of the club member, was busy bringing smiles on each kid's face when suddenly a little girl approached her and handed over her goodies' bag. Kshama politely asked her to take the bag as it was meant for all the kids. With a glorious smile, the girl beautifully replied- "No ma'am you do a lot for us. So, even I want to gift you something! Please have it as I've poured my love into it!" Her reply left everyone in awe and established the belief “DO GOOD, GOOD WILL AUTOMATICALLY COME TO YOU”, as ever strong.

Cleanliness Drive

So, It was Sept. 30th, 2019! A regular Monday for most, but a very important day for Team SOCH- Kuch Kar Dikhane Ki. While most of the people were busy dawdling about Monday blues, SOCH - the quintessential social enterprise of JECRC was all set with mops and brooms, to tide it all!!! These little soldiers put on the masks, stretched their gloves and were all prepared. After all, it was time for the much-awaited Annual CLEANLINESS DRIVE. When the team reached Statue Circle, there was little to no crowd, and as they started mopping the place, cleaning every nook and corner, to their surprise, none other than the social reformer of the area, Shri PD Sharan showed up!! Nor did he just motivated the team to work harder but he himself volunteered to clean the area.

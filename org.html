ORGANISATION

ZARURAT


"The Help Beyond"

Zarurat is a student-led social club of JECRC University founded in 2011. Our main motto is to provide an elementary level of education to the children who are not privileged enough. Zarurat is the path for children's well being and also provides the wealth of education at the same time. Children are the future leaders of tomorrow's tech-savvy world. So it is better to provide them with a platform where they can gain knowledge and develop themselves in the right direction. Keeping this as our mindset Zarurat is trying to provide help to the part of the society where the need is required. For now, Team Zarurat is constantly providing education even in the times of covid pandemic to the children who are living near the atmosphere of JECRC University, Jaipur.

Not only Zarurat focuses on academics but also focuses on the overall development of the child through various co-curricular activities which help them to build a strong and creative mind. We also organize many fun related activities for the children to keep them motivated. Zarurat is all about spreading happiness on the sweet faces of children under its wings.

  • Since 2011 with 7000+ working hours 5000+ Students have been mentored by the volunteers of Zarurat.


  • Made Limca book of records for making the largest origami flower basket.

  • Made Rajasthan book of records for 5 x 11-metre portrait of Mahatma Gandhi and Lal Bahadur Shastri.

SOCH


"Soch kuch Kar Dikhane Ki"

SOCH is a student held social organisation. It was established in 2014, The team is very cooperative and have a desire to do something good for their society and that is why SOCH is capable of working on more than one social issue which includes Cleanliness Drives, CPR training, Vastra Samman, Orphanage - Old age home visits. Being a multi-purpose Social club It focuses on every aspect of our society.

Saving mother Earth, lifting people above poverty, advancing economic growth are a few of the challenges SOCH is currently tackling. The team is constantly working to connect the dots between climate change, water scarcity, energy shortages, global health issues, and food security.

  • We have conducted many cleanliness drives at different places including our city Jaipur which throughout motivated 15000+ people.


  • Vastra Samman is a public event organised by us in which more than 10,000 clothes and goodies were distributed.

  • We have a Trained army for CPR of more than 500 students trained by renowned doctors.

SUHASINI


"Save the Smile of a Girl Child"

"When you educate a man you educate a man, when you educate a woman you educate a Generation."

It was back in the year 2015 when the scholars of JECRC took a collective initiative to consciousness the society towards the Education of Girl Child. The social group aimed to create a society with ever-smiling girl children, thus the initiative was named Suhasini meaning ”ever-smiling”.

One of the vertical columns under the umbrella of abhyudaya is Suhasini with the motto of "Saving and spreading smiles of a Girl Child." Suhasini is extremely determined in uplifting the education of girl children. For the same, Suhasini has adopted more than 5 villages and is also monitoring 12+ Government schools. Having a healthy engagement of volunteers and mentors the social column has inspired more than 220 girls to go to school.

  • More than 220 girls were motivated and inspired to go to school regularly.


  • More than 12 Government schools are under monitoring to ensure the quality of Education for the Girl Child.

  • A large number of girls were educated on Menstrual Hygiene.

AASHAYEIN


The Life Saviours

The foundation of Aashayein was led in the year 2006 by a group of geeks of JECRC. It is a social initiative with a motto to meet the demand of blood shortage. It is one of the social columns under the umbrella of Abhyudaya which works 24*7.

“Nobody can do everything, but everyone can do something"

Aashayein is also known as "The Life Saviour" for its consistent work from the past 15 years. The team has been working 24*7 to meet the demand of blood shortage. Every year the team Aashayein is responsible for organizing blood Donation Camps and it is the result of their hard work and determination that more than 16,667 units of blood are collected in the annual Blood Donation Drive. The team also holds the responsibility on their shoulders to arrange live Single Donor Platelets, donors, for the people who are in desperate need of SDP. More than 1400+ lives are saved through these SDP donations. With a selfless aim of saving lives the team of Aashayein has always made JECRC feel proud of them.

  • To date, more than 17,000 units of blood are collected through various Blood Donation Camps.


  • More Than 1400 lives were saved through live SDP Donations.

  • Also, the team pays a visit to Old Age Home to make sure that our senior citizens are happy and are living a tension free life.

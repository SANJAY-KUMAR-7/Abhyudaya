Our Events & Activities

Events

Mask Distribution

The Navratri festival is coming to an end, and Team Zarurat as always, never misses an opportunity to make the students feel every inch of happiness. This time we welcomed our freshers alongside our students and celebrated the festival with utmost joy by playing a fun activity. We disturbed masks and sanitizers to our students because we all know there are no smiles without safety.

Pre-Rhythm

Zarurat's Pre-Rhythm is an event that is full of excitement and fun. Children from various NGO's like Umaaang also join the event and witness the adrenaline rush. Also, it acts as a platform for the children under the wings of Abhudaya to showcase their hidden talents through dance performance, singing and much more. It's all about Dance, Music, Talks and Celebration.

Vastra Samman

Vastra saman is one of the biggest events of SOCH. It is a 10 day-long event involving a lot of participation from our scholars of JECRC. This event mainly focuses on spreading smiles on the face of small children. Donated clothes are distributed to the unprivileged ones.

Felicitaion Day

Felicitation day is one of the biggest events of the social vertical Aashayein. In this event, the social group awards the people who donated their blood to the Annual Blood Donation Camp. The motto of this event is to motivate people to donate their blood. It is a way of thanksgiving given to all the people who thought of saving a life and donated blood.

Celebrating Innocence

"Celebrating Innocence" is an annual fest organized by Zarurat on 14th March every year it is one of the prestigious societies which has been constantly working towards the enlightenment of young minds often deprived of basic education.

Old-Age Home Visit

Life is lived by all but the difference comes from how the person lived it. The ways vary as some are rich and some are poor but we have forgotten that everyone has a right to live a happy life but each and everyone has their definition of it. Team Aashayein have one for them too by visiting old age home. From visiting residents for hours, playing games with them, enjoying meals to listening to their lives experience Team Aashayein completes their successful day.

International Girl Child Day

These two days are celebrated with great pleasure by team Suhasini. They try to create more and more awareness among the people about girl child on this day. They tell how a woman plays an important role in one's life by many plays and rallies. Through the art of dance, plays etc they try to create a beautiful impact on these days.

Cleanliness Drive - Statue Circle

Cleanliness is the state of being clean. Staying clean is as important as eating, sleep, drink etc. Keeping the environment clean should be a daily habit of a human being. Team Soch runs much regular cleanliness drives in Jaipur not only to clean the point but also they can provide the proper awareness of people towards a healthy and clean environment. Team Soch has run drives on statue circle, many parks, many public areas so that they can aware people at their best as cleanliness of any kind have equal importance.

Diya Decoration

Diyas are a symbol of hope and light and Zarurat's Diya Decoration Event is one of its kind. Every year students from our institute along with all the faculty members gather at the centre lawn and even people from outside the University gather to decorate the Diyas. These diyas are then sold to students and the collected money is used to make the Diwali of these children full of lights.

Orphanage Visit

Sometimes we have many reasons to be happy at the same time when others haven't. Giving own happiness to another is not possible but spreading happiness to them is in our hands. Team soch also provides their hands to those sweet faces by visiting them and spend a whole day playing games with those children, watching their favourite cartoon, cooking their food, teaching them and do many extracurricular activities for their development.

Makkar-sakranti

Makar Sankranti is one of the biggest festivals in India. On this day we all gather with children at the Central lawn and have fun. We celebrate the festival with zest and joy. We distribute Kites and sweets to the children and together we fly kites. Kites represent our strength as they go higher and higher in the sky.

Joy Of Giving Week

Joy of giving is also one of its kind which is organised by Zarurat-the help beyond. It is a 7-day lasting event comprising a lot of co-curricular activities for the children under the benefit of Abhudaya. It is all about dance, music, poems, band performance and whatnot.

SDP Donation

SDP or Single Donor Platelets donation camps are also conducted by team Soch. Through these camps, we get the SDP of a person which is more potent than domain platelets and can prove more necessary than them. These type of camps and the proper use of SDP is not common nowadays, so it is the initiative taken by team Soch so we can create more and more awareness among people and tells about its importance.

Story Behind Red

This is the camp conducted by team Suhasini in various schools, locality to promote awareness regarding the menstruation cycle among girls. This camp collectively tells the girls about the menstruation cycle and how it appears. Not only this we tell them to choose PADS OVER CLOTHES so that they can know the benefits of using it.

Nukkad Natak

Nukkad Natak is organised by Suhasini. It is all about roadshows, motivating people for the education of girl child. Every year Suhasini organise such roadshows with positive ideas and thoughts throughout Jaipur city last time it was organised at Albert Hall.

Survey's By Suhasini

Suhasini also holds the Responsibility to conduct surveys regularly. They visit government schools and perform their survey. Their data includes a number of children studying, quality of education, quality of mid-day meal food and also the count of girl children in a particular school. These data are used for making future target plans.

School Visit

Suhasini also holds the Responsibility of visiting government schools for providing the help possible to the kids. They visit government schools and ask if there is any help we can offer. To date, we have more than 12 schools under monitoring.

Bird Feeder

The scorching heat of the summer season makes it more difficult for the birds to get their food or water. After seeing a lot of pigeons dying due to scarcity of water, our young minds from Zarurat decided to install bird feeders throughout the campus. Also, our scholars make sure to fill them every day with fresh water.

CPR Event

CPR event is one of the biggest and most successful events organised by Soch. It is a workshop where renowned doctors visit the campus to train young minds on the technique of CPR. To date, we have a trained army of more than 500 students of our institute trained in CPR.
